Our Pleating Curtain Systems

Changing living standards in recent years have allowed people to spend more time at home. In this context, people are making a number of initiatives in order to beautify their living spaces and lead a more comfortable life. One of the most important is the Pleated Curtain (Glass Balcony Curtain). With the name of Glass Balcony Curtain, the area of use is much more. It has two important characteristics. The first is that it cuts off the sun's rays in undesirable situations, and the other important feature is that it closes the image in undesirable situations. It adds a different atmosphere to the spaces with different color and different fabric options. AK Pleating Company is one of the leading companies in the sector with its rich color and fabric options. The whole system and its components are its own patented product.

Hira Series

It is our series that is called Black Out in the industry. 100% image closure is provided. It is produced Decently by placing a special Teflon material between two different fabrics. Currently, 4 colors are available. Due to the double color fabric, double-sided colors can be used in different colors,one of the most important features is that it helps to cut off the sun's rays by applying it to the ceiling, especially in the winter garden systems preferred abroad, it is one of our most preferred series.
