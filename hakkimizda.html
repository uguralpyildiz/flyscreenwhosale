About us

In our office located in ZenPlise Istanbul Sancaktepe, we maintain our leading position in the sector in the production and sale of pleated flapper and pleated curtain materials. In addition to high-quality materials, our company provides a comprehensive service by providing all the necessary machines and apparatus in the production process to our customers.

Our comprehensive product range has been carefully selected and developed in order to provide the most suitable solutions for the needs of our customers. Whether for homes or commercial spaces, our pleated fly screens and curtains, which offer aesthetics and functionality Decoupled, make your living spaces more comfortable.

Technology

Our mission

As ZenPlise, our mission is to increase the comfort and aesthetics of living spaces by offering high-quality pleated fly screen and pleated curtain materials to our customers. With our advanced technology production facility and expert team, we are working to provide the fastest and most effective solutions to the demands of our customers, to apply the highest quality standards in the industry and to keep customer satisfaction at the highest level.

Our Vision

ZENPLISE's vision is to become a nationally and internationally recognized and preferred brand in the pleated flapper and pleated curtain industry. We aim to strengthen our leading position in our sector with our continuous innovative approaches, customer-oriented service approach and high quality standards. With our environmentally friendly production methods and technological investments, we aim to add value to living spaces by offering sustainable and long-lasting solutions.

Characteristics of Our Production Area

Spacious and Modern Facilities

our production area of 5000 m2 is equipped with large capacity production lines and modern equipment. In this way, we are able to produce in high quantities and respond quickly to the demands of our customers.

Advanced Technology

We use the latest and most advanced technologies in our production processes. We make production in accordance with high precision and quality standards with plastic injection molding machines, folding machines and plastic extruder machines.

Professional Team

Our team consisting of engineers who are experts in their field and experienced employees manages the production processes meticulously. By carrying out quality control at every stage, we ensure that our products comply with high standards.

Environmentally Friendly Applications

We attach great importance to environmentally friendly applications in our production facility. By using energy efficient systems, we minimize our environmental impacts.

Safety and Occupational Health

The safety and health of our employees are one of the top priorities for us. We work in accordance with the highest safety standards in our production area and strictly comply with occupational health rules.

Our Production Capacity

Our production area in Tuzla, Istanbul, offers fast and effective solutions to all the demands of our customers with its high production capacity. With our wide product range in pleated flapper and pleated curtain materials, we are able to produce for all kinds of needs.

Our 5000 m2 Professional Production Area in Tuzla, Istanbul

With our service approach focused on quality, innovation and customer satisfaction, we are operating in our 5000 m2 full professional production area located in Tuzla, Istanbul.
