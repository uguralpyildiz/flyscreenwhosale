Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pleated flapper and how does it work?

Pleated flapper is a type of flapper that can be easily closed when not in use thanks to its foldable structure and prevents flies and other pests from entering when opened. It is extremely easy to use and is mounted on window or door frames.

What is a pleated curtain?

Pleated curtains are types of curtains that can be opened and closed by folding and offer a modern, stylish look. It offers effective solutions for light control and privacy.
