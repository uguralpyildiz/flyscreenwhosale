Get to Know Us

About Us

ZenPlise continues to lead the market with the production and sale of pleated fly screens and pleated curtain materials from our office in Sancaktepe, Istanbul. In addition to high-quality materials, we provide all necessary machinery and equipment for the production process, offering a comprehensive service to our customers.

Our extensive product range is carefully selected and developed to offer solutions that best meet our customers' needs. Whether for homes or commercial spaces, our pleated fly screens and curtains combine aesthetics and functionality to make your living spaces more comfortable.

Quality

Our products and equipment are manufactured according to high-quality standards.

Technology

We use the latest technology production machines.

Full Support

We provide all the machines and equipment you need during the production process.

Our Mission

At ZenPlise, our mission is to enhance the comfort and aesthetics of living spaces by offering our customers high-quality pleated fly screens and pleated curtain materials. With our state-of-the-art production facilities and expert team, we aim to provide the fastest and most effective solutions to our customers' demands, implement the highest quality standards in the industry, and maintain maximum customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

ZenPlise's vision is to become a nationally and internationally recognized and preferred brand in the pleated fly screen and pleated curtain sector. With our continuously innovative approaches, customer-focused service philosophy, and high-quality standards, we aim to strengthen our leading position in the industry. Through environmentally friendly production methods and technological investments, we strive to add value to living spaces by offering sustainable and long-lasting solutions.
We take pride in delivering our high-quality products to customers all over the world through our exports to global markets. If you are looking for a reliable supplier worldwide, contact us now.

Features of Our Production Area

Spacious and Modern Facilities

Our 5000 m² production area is equipped with high-capacity production lines and modern equipment. This allows us to produce in large quantities and quickly respond to our customers' demands.

Advanced Technology

We use the latest and most advanced technologies in our production processes. With plastic injection machines, folding machines, and plastic extruder machines, we produce with high precision and in accordance with quality standards.

Professional Team

Our team of expert engineers and experienced workers meticulously manage the production processes. By conducting quality control at every stage, we ensure that our products meet high standards.

Eco-Friendly Practices

We place great importance on eco-friendly practices in our production facilities. By using systems that ensure energy efficiency, we minimize our environmental impact.

Safety and Health

The safety and health of our employees are top priorities. We ensure that necessary occupational safety and health measures are fully implemented in all our production processes.

Production Capacity

Our production area in Tuzla, Istanbul, offers high production capacity to provide fast and effective solutions to all our customers' demands. With a wide range of pleated fly screens and curtain materials, we can meet any requirement.

5000 m² Professional Production Area in Tuzla, Istanbul

With our quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction-focused service approach, we operate in our 5000 m² fully professional production area in Tuzla, Istanbul. Our modern production facility, equipped with the latest technologies, enables us to maintain a leading position in the industry for producing pleated fly screens and pleated curtain materials.
