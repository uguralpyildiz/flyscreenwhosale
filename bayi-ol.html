Frequently Asked Questions

What is a plisse fly screen and how does it work?

A plisse fly screen is a type of screen that can be easily closed when not in use thanks to its foldable structure, and when opened, it prevents insects and other pests from entering. It is very easy to use and is mounted on window or door frames.

What is a plisse curtain?

Plisse curtains are a type of curtain that can be folded open and closed, offering a modern and stylish appearance. They provide effective solutions for light control and privacy.

Where can I purchase your products?

You can order our products directly through our website or obtain them from our sales points. For detailed information, please contact us.

Is it difficult to install plisse fly screens and plisse curtains?

No, the installation of plisse fly screens and plisse curtains is quite easy. By following the installation guides provided with our products, you can easily install them. If you need assistance with the installation, feel free to contact our support team.
