Bird's Eye Punching and Fastening Machine

Bird's Eye Punching Machine; It is a special machine used in the production of Fly Screens and Curtains.When producing Flapper and Curtain,special taped strips are used that ensure that the tulle and curtain remain stable. Thanks to this machine, the punching process of tulle, curtain and ribbon is applied.Thanks to its special Mold, symmetrical holes are opened to the ribbon tulle and curtain in the size of a Bird's Eye.

The Bird's Eye Fastening Machine is the same machine as the punching machine.The only difference is that their patterns are different.The Bird's Eye Lighting process is applied with this machine. The punching process that we performed the punching process on our previous machine is performed by ramming with this machine and the installation of the tulle and curtain to the strip is completed.Then the tulle and curtain process becomes ready to be collected(roped).

Rope Track Punching Machine

Rope trail tulle and curtain punching machine is an important machine necessary for the production of mosquito nets and curtains.We can also call it a column drill.Thanks to its special inserts and molds, it is ensured that the tulle and curtain are pierced in a symmetrical way without problems. The machine is fixed on the production machine.The punching process is performed with a specially prepared mold parallel to the ribbon meter and the front of the machine

Curtain Rope Adjustment Machine

Stringing MACHINE: After the curtain production is made, the curtain installation is made according to the special size. The most important feature of this machine is that it makes the curtain system ready for installation thanks to the special tape system after the curtain production is completed. The curtain is placed on the tape system and the clips are locked according to their size with the measuring rail on it. During installation, the clips are placed in their places only by screwing or gluing, and the installation process is completed

Rope (Rope)

Pleated Flapper Pvc Taped Strip is glued to the tulle of the flapper and provides the connection between tulle and aluminum. The biggest feature of this product is that it is made of heat-resistant Pvc material. The double-sided tape on it does not lose its properties in outdoor weather conditions, heat and cold. After the Pvc Strips are produced, their durability under hot weather conditions is checked by our company and tapes specially produced for this product are glued.
