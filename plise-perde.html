Pleated Curtain Systems

In recent years, changing living standards have allowed people to spend more time at home. In this context, people have been making various efforts to beautify their living spaces and lead a more comfortable life. One of the most important of these is the Pleated Curtain (Glass Balcony Curtain). The term Glass Balcony Curtain indicates a much wider range of use. It has two main features. The first is its ability to block sunlight in unwanted situations, and the other is its ability to obscure the view in unwanted situations. With various color and fabric options, it adds a different atmosphere to spaces. AK Plise Firmasi is one of the leading companies in the industry with its rich color and fabric options. All systems and components are their own patented products.

Lina Series

This is the most important series in our portfolio of 40 colors. It offers a wide range of 16 colors, with a coverage and opacity rate of 55-60%. It is the most preferred series due to its competitive price. Its unique pattern provides an aesthetic appearance to your spaces.

Sun Series

We can describe this series as the closest to our Hira series (Black Out) in terms of light-blocking ratio. The blackout ratio is around 90%, and it is available in four colors. The fabric structure is thicker compared to other series. Black Out fabrics are a more economical alternative for spaces where blackout fabrics are applied.

Crescent Series

This series has similar features to our Lina series. It stands out with its unique texture pattern. It is available in four popular colors. Cream and Anthracite Gray are particularly preferred.

Spring Series

This series has a fine and dense weave structure. It features a slightly shiny surface. It is a frequently preferred series in projects. It is often chosen for windows due to its color harmony with PVC frames.

Vega Series

This series features a unique weaving pattern referred to as knitting. It has similar features to our Crescent series. It is frequently chosen for Winter Gardens due to its special colors.
